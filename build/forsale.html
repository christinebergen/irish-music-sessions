3D Printed Custom Made Whistles

Irish Whistles are a great starter instrument for those wanting to dip their toes into the world of Traditional Irish Music. The low monetary investment, compact size, and ease of playing really make these the perfect starter instrument. One of our regular Irish Whistlers, Tony, is an experienced musician with many years playing Traditional Irish Music. He has perfected the design to be a durable instrument with a lovely sound. These 3D printed custom made whistles are available in any colour or style you desire and can be made to play in almost any key. Custom accessories are also available, such as whistle stands and cases to keep them in.

These whistles start at $15 and can be ordered by contacting Tony directly: tonemaker3D@gmail.com
