Our purpose is to hold Traditional Irish Music Sessions at local venues in the Nanaimo area.

These sessions are modeled after the Traditional Irish Music Sessions found in pubs all over Ireland. The format is fairly informal, usually just a bunch of musicians sitting together and playing some tunes.

If you're interested in playing with us, we suggest coming to a session to hear what we sound like. With thousands of Traditional Irish Tunes to choose from, we have developed a core list of tunes to help you get started. Visit the Tune Book page to see a list of some of the tunes we play.

No two sessions are the same as we have a few regular muscians but also various musicians who join us when they can, and a new face or two almost every session. The variety of tunes also varies greatly depending on the musicians who show up, and that's something we love about our sessions!

Our sessions are open to all musicians who already play or want to learn to play Irish Traditional Music, although traditional Irish instruments are preferred. If you're interested in joining us, we encourage you to come watch a session and say hello!

We respectfully acknowledge that we gather on the traditional and unceded territories of the Coast Salish peoples, including the territories of the Snuneymuxw, Snaw-naw-as, and Stzuminus First Nations.
